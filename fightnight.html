Qualtrics hosted a charity dance battle event at their 2019 X4 event. I was given control over the 100'x25' screen as well as LED bars hanging around the room.

The visuals are created live with a custom built Touchdesigner application controlled by a Novation Launch Control XL. The effects are a mix of 3D renders, 2D animation, as well as video loops with real-time image processing.
